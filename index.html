Character Recognition

help

Press any letter on your keyboard to render it into the "input" box. Capital letters and numbers work too.

You can also draw in the box. Left mouse button is pen, right mouse button is eraser. Pressing space clears the box.

First, click "train" to train the perceptrons. Then, to make the perceptrons classify the input, click "classify" or press enter. To make it harder to classify, you can click "add noise".
